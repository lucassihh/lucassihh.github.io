Tips

1

Practice pronunciation daily for better accent

Spend 10-15 minutes each day focusing on pronunciation. Use voice recording apps to compare your pronunciation with native speakers.
2

Use flashcards for memorization

Create digital or physical flashcards with the indonesian word on one side and english translation on the other. Review them during free moments.
3

Listen music and podcasts

Immerse yourself in the language through entertainment. Start with slow-paced content and gradually increase difficulty.

15 minutes daily is better than 2 hours once a week!

family and friends Basics

To say "father" in Indonesian, the word is Ayah

The word for "mother" is Ibu

To say "brother," you can use the phrase Saudara laki-laki

The phrase for "sister" is Saudara perempuan

To say "son," the phrase is Anak laki-laki

The phrase for "daughter" is Anak perempuan

To say "grandfather," you use the word Kakek

The word for "uncle" is Pamanah

To say "aunt," you use the word Bibi

The phrase for "cousin (male)" is Sepupu laki-laki

To say "cousin (female)," you use Sepupu perempuan

The phrase for "friend (male)" is Teman laki-laki

To say "friend (female)," you use Teman perempuan

The word for "husband" is Suami

To say "wife," the word is Istri
